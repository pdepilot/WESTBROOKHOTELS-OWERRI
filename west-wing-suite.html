Welcome to the West-Wing Suite, the crown jewel of WestBrook Hotel. Spanning an impressive 185 square meters, this magnificent suite occupies the entire west wing of our top floor, offering unparalleled panoramic views of Owerri's skyline. Every detail has been meticulously crafted to provide an experience that transcends traditional luxury, creating a sanctuary of sophistication and comfort.

The suite features a grand living room with hand-selected Italian furniture, a private dining area for eight, a fully equipped kitchen with marble countertops, and a master bedroom with a custom-made king-size bed dressed in the finest Egyptian cotton linens. The opulent marble bathroom includes a deep soaking tub, a separate rain shower, and dual vanities.

Your personal butler is available 24/7 to cater to your every need, from arranging private dining experiences to coordinating exclusive excursions around Owerri.
