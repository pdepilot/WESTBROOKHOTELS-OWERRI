Welcome to WestBrook

Discover the story behind Owerri's premier luxury destination

Redefining Luxury Hospitality

Nestled in the heart of Owerri, Imo State, WestBrook Hotel stands as a testament to luxury, elegance, and unparalleled Nigerian hospitality. Since our inception in 2014, we have been committed to creating exceptional experiences for discerning travelers from around the world.

Our journey began with a vision to establish a sanctuary that blends contemporary luxury with authentic Nigerian warmth. Today, we are proud to be recognized as one of West Africa's premier luxury destinations.
